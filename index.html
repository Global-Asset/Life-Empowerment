EFIKCOIN (EFC) — Architecture of Giving

Efikcoin is a deflationary BEP-20 token on Binance Smart Chain with a total supply of 1,000,000,000 EFC. It combines DeFi primitives — staking, liquidity provision, and governance — with a hard-coded charitable giving mechanism that cannot be removed by any actor, including the original deployer.

A portion of every DEX transaction fee flows automatically to the treasury wallet, which funds verified humanitarian programs across Africa, Asia, and the Americas. All flows are publicly auditable on BSCScan.

EFC holders stake in four tiers, claim rewards at any time, and participate in governance votes on treasury allocation. In the beginning everything was created, and he who created the world beautified it and blessed it — so too shall EFC bless the world. 🕊️
